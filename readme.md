# 👋 Hi, I'm Makhmud Bobokandov

**Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics | Senior Lecturer & Researcher**

I am a researcher at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computer Analysis, **National University of Uzbekistan (NUUz)**. 
My work integrates theoretical analysis and numerical modelling of nonlinear physical processes, specialising in the mathematical foundations of second-order parabolic PDEs, self-similar solutions, and blow-up phenomena.

---

### 🔬 Research Focus
My primary research involves the qualitative and numerical study of **nonlinear second-order parabolic PDEs**, specifically:
* **Double Nonlinear Equations:** Investigating non-divergence form equations with variable density.
* **Asymptotic Behaviour:** Analysing self-similar solutions and blow-up phenomena in inhomogeneous media.
* **Reaction-Diffusion Systems:** Modeling biological populations and cross-diffusion systems.
* **Numerical Methods:** Developing robust solvers for deterministic physics-based models.

### 🎓 Teaching & Mentorship
